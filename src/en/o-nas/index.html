About us

We have a dream of green, flowering and self-sufficient Prague that is supplied by urban and suburban organic farms, gardens, vegetable patches and orchards. Where it’s possible for anyone interested to participate in food production and landscape cultivation. The surrounding agricultural landscape no longer serves only food production, but also promotes biodiversity, retains water in the landscape, captures carbon dioxide and other greenhouse gases in the soil and plants, and where people can relax. With new jobs being created that enable social inclusion.
