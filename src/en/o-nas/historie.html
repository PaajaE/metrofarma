History

Our project was born in the spring of 2019 on an abandoned brownfield in Prague-Holešovice in the emerging Holešovice Leisure Centre.

Štěpán, who founded the project, bought a caravan and became a permanent resident of the garden. We started with a couple of the first patches and five hens rescued from factory farming. Over time, more people and animals were joined us, and in 2020 we already had 70 hens, a flock of sheep and dairy goats buzzing hives, quails and over 100 enthusiastic gardeners.

In the summer of 2020, we were shaken by an unexpected termination notice from the landlord. The search for a new space began. The City Hall gave us a helping hand and in a few months we found a large space on the nearby Císařský ostrov, where our garden is now located.

Next year, in 2021, people from the surrounding area quickly reserved all 170 individual patches. When Vendy came, a community field was created, which not only fed us during the season, but also produced the first vegetables and fruits for sale. In the autumn of 2021, our garden was too small for us, so we began to prepare our extension to fields and pastures in Jinonice where we are not only building another community garden, but also learning field farming and grazing goats and sheep on the extensive meadows around the Prokop Valley.
