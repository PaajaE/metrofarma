MetroPole Jinonice

The MetroPole project is a combination of a community garden and an urban farm. The total area of the field is about 1.8 hectares. We have enough space and welcome people interested in individual as well as community farming. We also welcome volunteers to help us with the various activities associated with building and running our new farm. Our project is being created primarily with the support of the City Hall of Prague on whose land we operate and with the support of the City District of Prague 5.
