Individual garden patch

An area of about 8,000 square meters is set aside for individual patches of 1– 200 square meters. If you follow the rules of organic farming, you can farm on your field as you please. Shared hand tools and small motor machines will be available to you during farming, as well as information and advice from the main farmer. We’ll also share seeds and planting material.

You can also expect a field common area, a fence to protect your crops or a campfire.

I am interested in a field

What will grow there?

We have to adjust the composition of plants and the method of farming based on natural irrigation possibilities in the form of rain. We will therefore mostly grow potatoes, legumes, corn, squash (last year we even harvested ripe tomatoes), as well as various types of shrubs, herbs and flowers.

Membership

What does it include?

  • Gardening tools and small machines
  • Advice, information and coordination from the main farmer
  • Organic material such as manure, grass, leaves, straw, etc. for an even better harvest
  • Space for growing in your chosen area
  • 10 mandatory hours of work on the community field per year.

Membership fee for 2022 is CZK 20/sqm/year

Minimum fee is CZK 500
