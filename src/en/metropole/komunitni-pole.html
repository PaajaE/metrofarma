Community Field

On the second part of the field on the so-called community field we farm under the leadership of the main farmer. Anyone can work on the community field. If you decide to participate, you will be rewarded accordingly by a share of the crops. In addition to the crops, you will also learn new farming knowledge and skills. We’ll also join forces with a small flock of hens, which won’t just keep us company, but they’ll also be invaluable garden helpers.

I want to participate in the community field

What will we grow?

In 2022, we are going to grow mainly potatoes, corn, squash, flowers and herbs.
