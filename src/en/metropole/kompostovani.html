Composting

The greatest gift for every gardener is black nutritious compost, which, when added to the garden patch, diversifies and supports soil life.

At our MetroPole we compost everything we can like grass, leaves, manure, wood chips and organic waste from the field and also from our homes.

We will be very happy if you support our future crop with organic waste from your home.

Shared compost

Where can you dump your organic waste?

  • At the highest point of our field you will find composting boxes (see picture).
  • Dump your organic waste into a box marked with a sign "Organic waste here".
  • The compost is accessible 24/7.

coffee grounds and tea leaves

toilet paper rolls, egg trays
meat leftovers
dairy products
