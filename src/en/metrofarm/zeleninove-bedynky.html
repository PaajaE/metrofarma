Veggie boxes

Do you want to receive regular seasonal fresh organic veggies from our farm? Would you like to support local production and economy and participate on the way your food will be grown? Join our crowd-farming

The pick-up point is MetroFarm, Prague 7, Císařský ostrov in Dejvice, near Vítězné náměstí.

If you are from Prague 6 or 7 and its surroundings and you are interested in regular veggie boxes from Metrofarm, you can sign up here.

How does it work?

How does it work?

Subscribe to veggie

  • The boxes run on a monthly subscription basis – register for one or three months and pick up your box between 4pm and 7pm every Tuesday and Friday when it’s freshly harvested.
  • The boxes are always freshly harvested, with a varied selection of vegetables that is always a bit different. The weight of the boxes varies seasonally based on the crops it contains.
  • The box contains 8–12 items that we are currently harvesting on the farm.
