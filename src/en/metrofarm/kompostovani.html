Composting

The greatest gift for every gardener is black nutritious compost, which, when added to the garden patch, diversifies and supports soil life.

We mostly compost grass and leaves from the Stromovka park, manure and bedding mainly from our goats and hens, as well as organic waste from the garden and from our households.

We prefer so-called fast hot composts which release beautiful smoke. When you toss it you strengthen the body and relax the mind.

We will be very happy if you also bring us organic waste from your home

Shared compost

Where can you dump your organic waste?

  • Next to the access road and in the middle of the garden behind the house we have several wire baskets. Dump the compost into a half-empty labeled basket.
  • The compost is accessible 24/7.
