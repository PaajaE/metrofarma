Individual garden patch

We have enough space and both complete beginners and seasoned farmers are welcome. Become a member and get your own garden patch.

I am interested in a garden patch

Basic membership

What does it include?

  • Approx. 5 square meters of growing space
  • Gardening tools
  • Organic material: manure, leaves, wood chips, grass, compost (which needs to be tossed from time to time)
  • Gardening workshops throughout 2022
  • Mandatory 10 hours of work a year at our community field, maintaining our garden or other help with our garden that you’ll enjoy. Working on a community field will not only give you new gardening skills, but you will also be rewarded with our veggie boxes or seedlings for your garden patch.
  • You need to maintain the space up to 30cm around your patch and mow it at least 3 times a year.
  • Garden patch certificate (on request)

Membership fee for 2022 is CZK 2,500

Or CZK 4,000 for a 10 sqm patch
