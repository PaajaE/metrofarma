MetroFarm Císařský ostrov

Our community garden and farm is thriving and growing. There are many ways to get your hands dirty and break a sweat during our farming adventures. We have individual garden patches, take care of animals, grow veggies on our community field and transform our common areas. New members, visitors and volunteers are always welcome.

Come take a look!

Individual garden patch

Something between your own garden and a window box. Get 5 or 10 square meters for gardening. Plan it, grow it, have fun with it. Enjoy growing your own food.

Community field

Grow your own food under the guidance of our chief gardener, gain new experience and get to know like-minded people.

Veggie boxes

Composting

Bring us organic waste from your home. Using nature’s magic, we’ll turn it into rich compost and use it to grow a tomato, a pepper or a melon.
