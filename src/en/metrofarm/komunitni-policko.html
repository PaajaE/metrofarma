Community field

Come to Císařský ostrov and grow your own veggies. The field combines a market garden where you can buy freshly harvested vegetables and flowers, with a community-supported agriculture model in which you can engage in growing your own food. The community field gives anyone who is interested the opportunity to get involved and learn something new under the guidance of an experienced gardener.

In addition to new gardening knowledge and skills, you will be rewarded for your work by veggie boxes from our field.

I want to get involved

How can I get involved?

Come to a work party

  • The easiest first step is to come to one of our regular work parties.
  • Our farmers (Vendy, Ginny and Terka) run social field meetings or work parties for anyone interested in learning the practical skills of growing their own organic food.
  • Work parties take place every Sunday from 4pm to 7pm and every Tuesday from 4pm to 7pm with a tea break and shared snacks.

What should I bring?

Mainly proper outdoor shoes and clothing. There are many ways to get dirty or sweaty. Feel free to bring something sweet or savory, hot or cold, big or small to share.
