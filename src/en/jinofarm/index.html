JinoFarm

Community project focused on animal farming and nature conservation. If you always wanted to rescue dying species in the Amazon but it didn't work out, don't despair! You can help us save dying insect species in Prague's Jinonice.

Come take a look!

How can I get involved

If you wanted to have a farm and raise livestock when you were little, but you live in Prague, come to us to make your dreams come true. We raise goats, sheep, rabbits and poultry. We milk, make cheese, learn, graze and relax with animals.
